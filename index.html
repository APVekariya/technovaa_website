Our Story

Founded in 2018, Technovaa emerged from a vision to bridge the gap between innovative hardware design and practical IoT implementations. What started as a small team of passionate engineers has grown into a leading technology solutions provider.

Our expertise spans across multiple industries, with specialized focus on solar energy systems and textile manufacturing automation. We've successfully delivered over 500 PCB designs and 50+ complete IoT solutions to clients worldwide.

Today, we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in embedded systems, combining cutting-edge hardware with intelligent software to create solutions that drive real business value.

To empower businesses with innovative embedded systems and IoT solutions that enhance efficiency, reduce costs, and drive sustainable growth through cutting-edge technology implementations.
